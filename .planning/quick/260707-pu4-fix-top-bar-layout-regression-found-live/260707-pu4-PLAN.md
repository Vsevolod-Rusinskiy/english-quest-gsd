<objective>
Fix a top-bar layout regression from quick task 260707-krq. The `.top-bar` is a single `display:flex; justify-content:space-between` row with no wrap and no flex-grow, now holding 6 elements. The `.progress-bar` (no explicit width/flex-basis) collapses to `width:0` and is invisible; the title/chips/text wrap awkwardly across 2-3 lines in a ~760px viewport.

Purpose: Restore a legible, non-collapsing top bar by restructuring it into two rows and giving the progress bar real width. Also stop the "освоено 0 / 0 тем" summary from showing before any topic has been touched.

Output: A two-row top bar (identity/reward row + progress row) with a visible full-width progress bar, and a topic-mastery summary hidden until at least one topic has an entry.
</objective>
